Our Story

Born in Kireka–Bbira, Crafted for Every Home.

Sopy Detergents began with a simple mission in the heart of Bbira: to create a cleaning solution that doesn't compromise. We believe that professional-grade cleaning power should be gentle on fabrics and safe for families.

Our journey from a local manufacturer to a trusted national brand is built on a foundation of consistency, innovation, and an unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction.
